ShramArogya Health for Migrants

Caring for Migrant Workers’ Health

Migrant workers form the backbone of Kerala’s economy, yet they often face limited access to healthcare. ShramArogya is a digital health initiative designed to track vaccination records, chronic conditions, and clinic visits of migrators. This ensures early detection, timely care, and overall better well-being for thousands of workers.
