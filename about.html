About ShramArogya

Empowering migrant workers with accessible health monitoring, while building secure datasets for informed government policy-making — all while keeping individual privacy protected.

Our Mission

To provide migrant workers with simple, reliable, and continuous health monitoring that ensures timely care and prevents health risks.

Our Vision

To create structured health datasets that help governments design effective health policies and improve the well-being of migrant communities.

Our Values

We believe in accessibility, inclusivity, and prevention. Our platform ensures every worker’s health matters and is accounted for.

Our Commitment to Privacy

All data collected through ShramArogya is stored with utmost care, ensuring confidentiality of each worker. Personal identities remain private, and only anonymized data is shared with government bodies to help shape health policies.
